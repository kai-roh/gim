# Architect Image Summaries

## le_corbusier
Name: Le Corbusier
Summary: Pilotis, free plan, ribbon windows, roof garden, rational modernist geometry, clean proportions, functional order, and bright open facade logic.
Materials: white reinforced concrete, painted concrete, clear glass, pale plaster, light steel detailing

## frank_lloyd_wright
Name: Frank Lloyd Wright
Summary: Organic architecture growing from the landscape, strong horizontality, layered roofs, extended planes, warm natural materials, and seamless continuity with the site.
Materials: warm stone, brick, wood, bronze metal, textured plaster, earthy glass tones

## mies
Name: Mies van der Rohe
Summary: Extreme geometric simplicity, steel and glass modernism, elegant proportional clarity, open universal space, and disciplined minimal expression.
Materials: steel, clear glass, dark metal, polished stone, refined mullions, precise structural joints

## gaudi
Name: Antoni Gaudi
Summary: Biomorphic architecture inspired by trees, bones, and natural geometries, with sculptural curves, expressive structure, and highly artistic spatial flow.
Materials: ceramic mosaic, textured stone, colored glass, carved concrete, sculpted metal, artisanal finishes

## kahn
Name: Louis Kahn
Summary: Monumental modernism with heavy material presence, timeless geometry, deep shadows, solemn silence, and powerful natural light.
Materials: brick, board-formed concrete, travertine, heavy stone, raw timber accents, bronze details

## aalto
Name: Alvar Aalto
Summary: Warm human-centered modernism with soft curves, Nordic natural materials, tactile interiors, and gentle regional light.
Materials: wood, pale brick, matte plaster, soft metal, warm stone, diffused glass

## ando
Name: Tadao Ando
Summary: Exposed concrete minimalism, pure geometry, contemplative silence, sculpted natural light, deep shadow, and introspective spatial sequence.
Materials: smooth exposed concrete, clear glass, muted metal, still water surfaces, crisp joints, minimal stone

## foster
Name: Norman Foster
Summary: High-tech architecture with visible engineering, lightweight structure, glass and metal skin, sustainable precision, and clean futuristic order.
Materials: high-performance glass, aluminum, stainless steel, composite metal panels, exposed structure, polished technical finishes

## hadid
Name: Zaha Hadid
Summary: Fluid parametric architecture, dynamic motion, non-linear surfaces, bold sculptural massing, and futuristic spatial continuity.
Materials: smooth GFRC, composite panels, glossy metal, seamless glass, high-finish resin surfaces, polished concrete

## koolhaas
Name: Rem Koolhaas
Summary: Program-driven architecture with urban complexity, stacked volumes, deliberate tension, hybrid programs, and intelligent metropolitan collision.
Materials: glass, concrete, perforated metal, industrial finishes, exposed structure, utilitarian curtain wall systems
